Top donor lists only those who have given $3,000 or more. ActBlue is an online fundraising platform used by Democratic candidates; WinRed serves the same function for Republican candidates. Contributions listed under these groups are from individual donors who used the platforms to make donations. Candidates do not have to itemize donations of less than $50 in campaign finance filings. Individuals, political action committees and party committees may give up to $2,075 for each the primary and general election, for a total of $4,150. Contributions made to a particular candidate that are earmarked or directed through an intermediary such as a PAC or a party committee are considered contributions to the candidate, and are subject to the contribution limits.
